## Phase Goal

**As a** job seeker, **I want to** have job leads automatically scraped, filtered, matched to my best resume, and applied to via email with a tailored cover letter and Telegram notification, **so that** I never miss an eligible opportunity.

<objective>
Build the scraper vertical slice: JobSpy wrapper that scrapes Indeed (and other boards) in a thread executor, Kalibrr scraper using httpx + BeautifulSoup4, and two FastAPI endpoints (/scrape-jobspy, /scrape-kalibrr) that n8n will call on a 4-hour schedule.

Purpose: INGEST-02 (Kalibrr) and INGEST-03 (JobSpy/Indeed) provide job leads beyond Gmail alerts. These endpoints return normalized job lead lists that n8n loops through and feeds to /ingest-lead.
Output: Two working scraper endpoints returning job lead arrays; zero-result detection for OPS-01.
</objective>
